# Product: VT Dining Ranker

A mobile-first web app for Virginia Tech students that surfaces the best-rated campus dining options in real time.

## Core Value Loop
1. Students see what's good, what's trending, and how long the wait is — right now.
2. They rate what they eat, feeding the ranking engine for everyone else.
3. Personalization (dietary profiles, past ratings, weather) makes recommendations increasingly relevant.
4. Social and gamification layers drive ongoing engagement.

## Key Features
- Real-time menu display with recency-weighted rankings per dining hall
- Trending feed (top 10 items by rating activity in the past 60 min)
- Dietary profile filtering (vegan, gluten-free, halal, allergens, etc.)
- Health scores and nutritional tracking
- Crowdsourced wait times
- Personalized recommendations (factors in weather, past ratings, preferences)
- Social layer: follow friends, activity feed, privacy controls
- Photo reviews with moderation
- Gamification: streaks, badges, weekly leaderboard
- 7-day meal planning with push notifications
- Hokie Passport (meal swipe) balance integration
- Menu item availability history and recurrence predictions
- Event-based dining specials from VT Dining Services staff

## Target Users
Virginia Tech students, including those with dietary restrictions and meal plans.

## Dining Halls Covered
West End, D2, Turner Place, Perry Place, Owens, Squires (and any other VT on-campus facilities).
